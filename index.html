Soft Landing

There are a lot of preparations that need to be taken into account when considering relocation; most importantly, securing your employment and finding decent residancy.

We offer you a tool to not only search and find both, but to make sure that your new job & home are close to each other as much as possible!

Bundle Up!

Bundles are neatly packeged offers you get when searching Immiget.

depending on your focus, each bundle contains one type of item (i.e. a job offer), and all possible adjacent matches for the other type of item (i.e. housing offers)

Visualize

To make it even more intuative, each bundle can be displayed on a map - centering around the focused item, while showing all corresponding matches surrounding it
